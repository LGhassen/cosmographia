Cosmographia model sources

The Dawn and Rosetta spacecraft models were created by Brian Jones and are used in Cosmographia by his permission.

The models of Vesta, Phobos and Deimos are from Peter Thomas of Cornell University.

The models of Proteus, Amalthea and Hyperion are by Phil Stooke. His web page has a list of links to resources for mapping small Solar System bodies.

Shape models of the asteroids below are from DAMIT (Database of Asteroid Models from Inversion Techniques).

  • 2 Pallas
  • 3 Juno
  • 5 Astraea
  • 8 Flora
  • 10 Hygiea

The model of Itokawa was derived from Hayabusa images by Robert Gaskell.
