Welcome!



Cosmographia is a realistic, true-to-scale model of our Solar System. There's a lot of space to navigate; use these basic mouse and trackpad gestures to explore our neighborhood:

· Drag with the mouse to trackpad to orbit the central object

· Right drag the mouse to pan the view

· Use the mouse wheel or trackpad scrolling to change the distance to the central object

· Single click an object to select it

· Double click an object to travel to it

· Right (secondary) click an object to show the object menu
