How Do I... ?

Change the central object?

There are three ways to change the object that you are following:

  • Right click on it to show the menu, then choose 'Set as Center'
  • Click on it to select it, then press ⌘C
  • Go to it by double clicking (or selecting it and pressing ⌘G)

Float above a fixed point on a planet's surface?

  • Right click on it to show the menu, then choose 'Set as Fixed Center', or...
  • Click on it to select it, then press ⌘B

Best view a spacecraft flyby?

The following steps will make the view behave like it does for the flybys in the list of Cassini Mission Highlights.

  1. Set the date to around the time of the flyby and pause time
  2. Go to the spacecraft by double clicking it.
  4. Right click on the flyby target planet to show the menu, then choose 'Track'
  6. Left drag to position the target planet where you want it; 'track' makes it maintain the same position in the view.
  7. Unpause time, adjust the time rate, and watch the flyby.
